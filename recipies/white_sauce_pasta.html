White Sauce Pasta

These grilled peaches with fresh whipped cream are so easy and so fast that it's impossible to believe they could be so delicious

Ingridients needed

  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons margarine
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 cube chicken bouillon, crumbled
  • 1 Â½ cups boiling water
  • 1 cup 2% milk
  • ground white pepper to taste

Steps to prepare

  1. Gather the ingredients
  2. Melt butter and margarine in a saucepan over medium-low heat.
  3. Add flour and crumbled bouillon; stir until well blended, 1 to 2 minutes.
  4. Continue to cook and stir until thickened and lightly browned, about 5 minutes.
  5. Increase the heat to medium and whisk in water until smooth.
  6. Stir in milk; cook and stir until hot and thickened, 2 to 3 more minutes.
  7. Season with white pepper.
